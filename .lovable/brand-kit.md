## Long bio (LinkedIn page description, press kit)

> Terraria Workshops is a craft-tourism social initiative based in
> Tétouan — one of Morocco's UNESCO World Heritage cities and a
> historic centre for artisanal craft. We partner with master potters
> in Tétouan, weavers in Midelt, and zellige, embroidery and gardening
> ateliers across the region to open their working studios to
> travellers.
>
> Workshops are hands-on, conducted in the artisans' own ateliers, and
> available in English, French, Spanish and Arabic. Every session
> directly compensates the artisan you learn from. To date we've hosted
> 700+ participants, work with 6+ master potters and have helped sell
> 1,200+ artisan-made products through our online store.
>
> We also partner with hotels and riads to deliver concierge-grade
> craft experiences for their guests, and run multi-day craft retreats
> and corporate team-building programmes.
